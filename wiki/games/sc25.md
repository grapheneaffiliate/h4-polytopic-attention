# SC25
**Status:** 0/6 unsolved | **Tags:** keyboard_click | **Baselines:** 39, 5, 32, 33, 66, 41

## Mechanic
Spell-casting game. Toggle 3×3 spell grid, cast spells (teleport/size/fireball), move player to exit.

## Win Condition
Player collides with exit sprite "pcohqadae" → triggers exit animation → next_level()

## Why Unsolved
- **Deepcopy BROKEN AND zero pixel change on ANY action** (even with fresh game instances). The closure bug is so severe that even reset+replay produces 0 pixel changes.
- Level 1 baseline=5 (trivially short) but can't test any approach.

## Spells
- `jzukcpajs` (Teleport): pattern [[T,F,F],[F,T,F],[F,T,F]], color 11
- `fpokrvgln` (Size Change): pattern [[F,T,F],[T,F,T],[F,T,F]], color 15
- `aprnrzeyj` (Fireball): pattern [[F,T,F],[F,T,F],[F,T,F]], color 6

## Actions
- ACTION1-4: move player (±4 or ±2 cells based on scale)
- ACTION6: click on spell slots (3×3 grid at specific positions) or spell info buttons

## Key Variables
- `spell_slot_active`: 3×3 bool grid
- `actions_taken`: counter (lose when > limit)
- `sykpecmoq`: action limit per level
- Energy packs: reduce action count by 10 on pickup
